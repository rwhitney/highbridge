September 12, 2006

 

Minutes- General Meeting. Mores Creek Ambulance

 

The meeting was called to order at 1900 hrs.

 

Members in attendance were: Sheena Labrum, Ron Dotson, Carol Dotson, Debbie Wells, Jason Christensen, Deb Arnswald, Dawn Best, Karen Acker, Terry Acker, Dirk Myers, Jan Peterson and Carolyn  Bohn.

 

Drawing was held for shift sign ups.  Ron Dotson won again.  Received gift card to Home Depot.

 

Minutes were unavailable from August general meeting.

 

Sheena Labrum gave Treasurer’s report.  Reported $5097.44 in account.  Report was approved by membership.

 

Carolyn Bohn reported EBCA meeting was cancelled.

 

Run reviews:

 

Debbie Wells reported on run on Robie Creek Rd.  Talked about radio problems and throttle control problems on ambulance.  Dawn Best spoke about problems with communications with State Comm. on Daggett Creek page out.  Also dog bite incident on Robie Creek Rd. was discussed.

 

It was decided that a group of MCA members needed to visit State Comm. to resolve issues.

 

Carolyn Bohn talked about having a meeting at Pizza Hut to discuss Service awards. Nominations were needed for shifts and member of the year.  Voting would be held at the October general meeting.

Jan Petersen gave a training presentation on “Care for Firefighters”.
