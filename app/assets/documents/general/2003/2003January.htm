MORES CREEK AMBULANCE REGULAR MEMBER MEETING

Minutes for January 16, 2003

 

Terry Acker called the meeting to order at 6:50 pm.

 

New Board Members for 2003- Terry reviewed from last month

            President- Terry Acker

            Vice President- Nancy Cooper

            Secretary- Cindy Drury

            Treasurer- Jennifer Haney

            Advisory Board- John McCarthy

            Fundraising- Beth Chandler, Maureen Ericksen, and ___

 

Goals for the Unit- Establish unity between the ambulance units in east Boise County.  Try to build up the Good Will and reduce the negativity.  Terry will talk with other leaders to find out what can be done.  The refresher class last week was a good start.  It had participants from MCA, Idaho City and Placerville. 

 

New members- Terry introduced the three new members- Joanne McCarthy, Harry Blodget and Peggy Blodget.  All 3 are currently taking the EMT class in Idaho City.

 

Summary of Advisory Board Meeting- by John McCarthy-

·         Starting February 15, 2003 State Communications will dispatch extrication.  Boise County will be out of the loop.

·         New Striker gurney has been approved by Boise County.

·         The laptops with GPS units will be installed in the ambulance by summer

·         SOPs- The advisory board has completed a Draft Copy of the SOPs for East Boise County.  Jennifer Haney will scan them and they will be available for review.  Please review them within 2 weeks.  Comments will be reviewed at the Advisory Board Meeting in February.

 

Treasurer’s Report- Jennifer Haney.

We have $5,251.30 in the bank.

 

Schedule Master- Terry proposed that we need a new schedule Master.  He asked if anyone in the meeting was interested.  No one jumped forward.  Second option- rotate the duties between members.  Terry asked if there are any EMTs available for this weekend.

 

Supply Manager- Maureen will handle all supplies, except for oxygen. Doug Trosky will still handle the oxygen.  Please call Maureen if you know of any supplies we need.

 

Team Leaders- We are looking for new Team Leaders.

 

Certificate- Terry presented a certificate to Beth Chandler for her contributions to the unit and for serving as the President for 2 years.  He also presented Beth with a certificate for Angles Bar and Grill.  Beth thanked those who helped her with the Poinsettia project- Jennifer, Peggy, John,
Blaine and others.  Beth said we received a very nice thank you letter from one of the families who received a food basket.

 

Ambulance Shed- The hide-a-key is now under the pot by the front door.  There is a new electronic thermometer in the ambulance (by the access panel for the oxygen tank). 

 

We will be ordering a new Striker Gurney within a few days.  It will take about 6 weeks for delivery, and when it arrives we will have a training session on its use.  The blood pressure cuff is in for service.

 

Call Reports- 1) Difficulty breathing in Clear Creek.  MCA was dispatched to the scene without an address, only with mileage.  The mileage was off and it was difficult to find.  The road/driveway was a mudhole.  2) Snowmobile accident in Atlanta area.  Gus and Carolyn responded in the middle of the night.  The 43-year-old woman had been drinking and was verbally abusive and combative.  She had abdominal pain and other injuries.  Atlanta crew rendezvoused with MCA.

 

New Business

Employer’s and Volunteers- Dave Haney spoke to the group about the fact that employers can fire employees that are late or do not show because they are on a call for a volunteer emergency service unit.  He asked member to call their legislators and ask them to support the issue.  Jennifer will send a draft to Terry.  About seven states have protection for volunteers EMS members.

 

Identification Cards- Terry and John will check into the requirements for MCA EMTs to carry identification cards.

 

Meeting adjourned at 7:30
