MORES CREEK AMBULANCE REGULAR MEMBER MEETING

Minutes for May 15, 2003

 

Terry Acker called the meeting to order at 6:55 pm. 

 

The minutes and Treasurer’s report were passed around for approval.  Treasurer’s report- $4172.62 in checking.

 

Old Business

 

Jumpsuits- talk to Karen if you do not have a jumpsuit and are interested in one.

Life Flight- membership cards were passed out.  Cost was covered by MCA.  Additional family members are $35.00.  Call the 1-800 number on the back of the card if you want to add additional family members.

 

Rummage Sale- How will MCA use the proceeds?

 

Schedule- there are a lot of open shifts.  Sign up for extra shifts if you can.  Memorial Day weekend is May 25, 26, and 27.

 

New EMTs must go on 3 calls with a lead EMT or be on 20 shifts before they sign up as the sole EMT on a shift.  Nine EMTs passed their practical exam last Saturday.  It will be about 6 weeks until they obtain the results for the written exam and their EMT cards.

 

Vaccinations- Drivers are now required to obtain 3 vaccinations.  Can get vaccinations at the Health Clinic in Idaho City or at Central District Health on Emerald St. in Boise.  Diane volunteered to give the shots, the Board will check into the details.  Drivers also need CPR- Health Care Providers for Professional Rescuers.

 

Calls-

 

Diane and others- responded to a motorcycle incident on Highway 21.  Driver was not wearing a helmet and received head trauma.  He is in critical care.

 

Driver Orientation- John has volunteered. 

 

Messenger-  we need someone to telephone the members without email (4 people) to relay any messages send out by email.

 

Schedule Master- any volunteers for June?

 

Patient Care Reports (PCR) – there have been some issues and PCRs will be a training topic soon.

 

Rummage Sale- anyone interested?  Beth will get reminders out in email.

 

Training- this months training will be conducted by Dr. Susan Dillon.

 

Change in Bylaws- the organization’s name of “Mores Creek Ambulance, MCA” was changed to “MCA Incorporated”.  Jennifer read the changes to the members.  Also changed the PO Box to 54 Burnett Dr.  John motioned to approve, Beth seconded and all 14 members in attendance approved.

 

Budget- includes new ambulance tires for the summer, oil change, etcetera.  New equipment includes a humidifier for the Oxygen and an auto Blood Pressure machine in the ambulance.
