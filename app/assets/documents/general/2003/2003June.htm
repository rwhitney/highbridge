MORES CREEK AMBULANCE REGULAR MEMBER MEETING

Minutes for June 19, 2003

 

Terry Acker called the meeting to order at 6:55 pm. 

 

New Members-drivers- Jim Armstrong, Paul Drury

Newly certified EMTs- Blaine Whitney, Cindy Drury, Diane Shoemaker, Harry Blodgett, Peggy Blodgett, Jim Bryant, Joan McCarthy, Ken Wells, and Terry Rousseau.  

 

Advisory Board Meeting- June meeting covered by Beth while John was out of town. 

  • SOP’s are going to be finalized within the month. 
  • Ambulance Computer Update- computer have been place in Idaho City and Placerville ambulances.  The county wants 6 people trained on MCA before they give the computer to MCA.  Commissioner voted to deny us information about previous calls because our PCR’s have been incomplete and do not meet their standards. 
  • Life flight and St. Luke’s Air have a reciprocity agreement for us and all patients.  Life flight is working on ride along.  Probably 2 slots a year. 
  • For ALS rendezvous the county prefers that we use MPA (on Cole & Victory) instead of Ada County because MPA does not bill back.  Ada County charges on calls MCA can’t collect on. 
  • Executive session- commissioners approved random & spot check drug testing for EBCA members.

 

T-shirts- Karen brought t-shirts in.  Gave shirts to new people first, and then asked if others wanted a second.

 

Laptop- several of us met with Vicky for training.  8 MCA members need to be trained.  Vicky can conduct training in our area or up in Idaho City.  Vicky may ask the County Commissioners to rescind restrictions on data contained on our laptop if our PCRs improve.
