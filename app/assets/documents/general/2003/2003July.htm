MORES CREEK AMBULANCE REGULAR MEMBER MEETING

Minutes for July 17, 2003

 

Terry Acker called the meeting to order at 6:35 pm. 

 

Treasurer’s report- we have about $3500 in our checking account.

 

Advisory board meeting- one issue of the meeting focused on accusations that were made against several MCA members.  The accusations were made by EBCA members and they had no proof to back them up.  Several MCA members spoke with respect to the accusations.  The Advisory Board decided that any future complaints must be made in writing and be signed by the complaintant.  The complaints need to be submitted to EBCA with evidence and witnesses.

 

Picnic August 9th- in Robie Creek Park.  Meat will be provided, bring side dishes.  Spouses are welcome, but no kids this time around.  Bring your own drinks and a softball mit.

 

Recognition- MCA would like to recognize Doug Trosky for his time & dedication to serving the unit.  Carolyn Bohn- is also recognized for her time & dedication to the unit.  Mietta was also the first to sign up on the calendar for six months running.  Go Mietta. 

 

Laptop- the laptop computer for the ambulance has arrived, but we do not yet have a cable and lock so it is not in the ambulance.  Those individuals not yet trained on the laptop need to do so ASAP.  Billie suggested we get training on the laptop every so often.  Most members felt it should be up to the individuals to stay up-to-date with how to use the laptop and software.

 

Air St. Luke’s arrived for training.
