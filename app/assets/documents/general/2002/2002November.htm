MORES CREEK AMBULANCE

GENERAL MEETING NOTES

NOVEMBER 21, 2002

 

START: Beth Chandler opened Meeting at 6:50pm.

 

HOLIDAY HOPE FUNDRAISER – Beth has made posters and ‘burma shave’ signs for the fundraiser. An article has been written for the Idaho World and they will be following the project all the way through. The poinsettias and wreaths will be sold the afternoon of 11/29 and all day 11/30. Although some have been presold, there will still be over 120 items to sell those two days and help is needed. Food donations will be accepted at the same time. Ride tickets will be given for large food donations. In addition, Micron will be donating turkeys and hams to the project. The grocery shopping for the food baskets will be 12/12 and 12/13. They will be put together at the shed. Anyone interested in assisting with this project, please contact Beth.

 

TRAINING – Karen Acker is setting up 24-hour refresher and CPR classes. She has requested weekend class dates but is awaiting replies to firm up the schedule. She reminded all that they are responsible for maintaining their own certifications. EMT classes start in January in Idaho City on Sundays from 9am to 5pm.

 

ADVISORY MEETING – John McCarthy and Karen Acker attended meeting and report following:

  • Received approval for ride ticket exchange for food donation
  • EMT classes to be funded through unused county ambulance funds
  • EMT classes to be held in both Idaho City and Lowman.
  • Authority given to spend carry-over money for budgeted items
  • No protocol was made regarding calling death at scene

 

CALL REPORTS:

  • Karen reported on medical callout. She asked that when LZ is required we call out WR Extrication. If for some reason this does not occur, extrication personnel can call State Comm and ask if help is needed or request to be dispatched. In addition, Ada County EMTs are willing to drive beyond county line to meet at scene whenever necessary.
  • John reported on an extrication call. He is stocking unit with hot packs, extra socks and warm blankets.

 

ELECTIONS –The following officers were elected:

            President                     Terry Acker

            Vice President                      Nancy Cooper

            Secretary                     Cindy Drury

            Treasurer                     Jennifer Haney

           

 

ADJOURNMENT: Karen moved, Billie 2nd a motion to adjourn at 7:35pm for training.

 

TRAINING: Terry Acker reported on Winter Preparedness and Karen Acker conducted training on C-Collars.
