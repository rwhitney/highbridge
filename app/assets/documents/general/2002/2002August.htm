CALL REPORTS:

  • Doug reported on pickup rollover off 100 ft cliff. WR extrication truck was requested. Situation was confusing. Call went from Elmore County to Boise County to State Comm to MCA. Total of 7 vehicles and Life Flight chopper were looking for truck. We were finally flagged down by area campers and directed to the accident scene. The 2 men refused treatment. Total of 14 volunteers plus Life Flight personnel responded.
  • Terry reported motorcycle in creek at mm 25. Although MCA was out of service that day, several from our area went by to assist (Terry, Karen, Dirk, John, Joan, Jim, Blaine, Dave & others). The cyclist was found face down in the water. MCA personnel packaged him and handed off to Life Flight. He was a Level 2 trauma and Life Flight was impressed with our unit. Attempts were made to contact Idaho City to call off their ambulance but were unsuccessful. Complaint has been filed by Idaho City ambulance because MCA status was out-of-service but EMTs still responded.
  • Terry & Doug reported on man falling off horse in Robie Creek area. Initially he was not breathing and had fallen on his head. He was laying face down and his wife had rolled him over. His vitals would go and stop. When Doug got some O2 in him it made a big difference. Ambulance showed up and took to Life Flight LZ at Robie Creek dock. Confusion when RCVFD and WRVFD were both toned out to setup LZ.
  • Beth reported on a jeep rollover from June. Evidently felony DUI charges have been filed against the driver and details have been requested from MCA.
