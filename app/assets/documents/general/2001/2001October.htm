RADIO TRAINING – Kevin of Mountain States Communications, discussed the proper use of the new Kenwood radios:

  • The antennae is not a handle---if you break the pin on the bottom of the antennae, you break the radio.

  • If you drop the radio in water, remove the battery & put it in a paper bag

  • B or C buttons activate night mode

  • Batteries need to be charged 6 to 8 hours

  • Don’t leave the radio on while it is in the charger

  • Batteries will last on average 10-12 hours

  • Drain the batteries all the way down---they build a small memory
