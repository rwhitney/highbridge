Meeting called to order at 7:00 pm.

 

Dirk Myers and Dawn Best have been appointed our new MCA Training Officers. We will discuss the new training schedule and training changes at the next meeting, and then start the changes in March.

 

Advisory Board items:

 

Gordon Ravenscroft is getting new O2 bottles for the new EMT’s.

 

We will be getting a new heater for the ambulance bay.

 

EBCA is now doing drug testing for any accident involving the ambulance when damages exceed $750.

 

The ambulance will transport free the family members of unit personnel if it is a spouse, a child under 18 living in the home, or a dependent child under 23 who is a student.

 

Continuing Education Credits Information

 

Free CE hours are available through classes taught by Steve Cole on the first Wednesday of every month at the fairgrounds, and the last Tuesday of the month at Meridian Fire Station #1.  Each training starts at 7:00pm.  For additional information on these and other trainings, email Steve Cole at rcole@adaweb.net or colemedic@hotmail.com.

 

Meeting adjourned at 7:15 pm for Documentation Training with Steve Cole.
