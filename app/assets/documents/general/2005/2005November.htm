November 2005 MCA General Meeting Minutes

Tuesday, November 8th

 

 

Called to Order at 7:15pm.

 

Approval of Treasurer’s Report: report passed around and approved.

 

Approval of October’s general meeting minutes: minutes passed around and approved.

 

Advisory Board Meeting Report

-         Idaho City Ambulance is still holding trainings on certain nights of the month at the Idaho City High School, if anyone is interested in attending.

-         There will be a new EMT class held in Idaho City, every Sunday for 16 weeks.  It is open to EMT’s, if you would like to go and sit in on a class and get CE hours.  Make sure the class coordinators know you are there and record your CE hours.

 

Old Business

-         Motion to set up our calendar with 6 hour shifts instead of twelve hour shifts: seconded and passed.  We will try to get the logistics set up so that we can start the new calendar on January 1st.  Reminder to everyone to SIGN UP!

-         Several people offered to send in the calendar to State Comm at the beginning of each month.

-         There is a virus in an email that has been sent to many of the members from ‘mcaallmembers’.  Don’t open this email, just get rid of it.

-         The shed radio is in Idaho, and should be here soon.  Terry will get a list of what channels we want on it to EBCA.

-         The GPS unit is not working and is getting repaired.

-         We have been informed that we need training on the National Incident Management System (NIMS)…we need at least 70% compliance.  There is a FEMA course online for NIMS.  Call Jo McCarthy for the web address if you are interested.  We are also looking into having someone come and train us on it.

 

New Business

-         We will be having our MCA Christmas Party on Friday, December 9th at 6:00pm at Terry & Karen Acker’s house.  We will have catered food, but bring your own refreshments.  Also, bring a dessert is you feel like it and a white elephant gift.

-         We will need some new officers for the unit.  Talk to a board member if you are interested in serving as an officer (i.e. supply officer, training officers, etc.)

-         CPR Class will be held on Saturday the 12th from 8-2pm at the shed.

-         We need to set up trainings for all of our new equipment, such as the ped’s AED and the LifeBlankets.

-         It was suggested that we go through the ambulance and pare down on the amount of equipment that we have in it.  The question of what we can do with extra equipment and how we can distribute it was tabled for the next meeting.
