New Business:

-         Personnel issues:  The questions asked were, “What makes a group?” and  “How can we improve?”.  Filling shifts is one of our biggest problems right now.

-         Some ideas for improvements were discussed, including a newsletter on the website or mailed with open forum, news, member bios, EBCA agenda, and birthdays/anniversaries.  Also discussed was changing the meeting structure so that the board meeting, general meeting and training were all on one night again.
