Old Business:

New Business:

-         We would like to do a survey of our MCA members to find out what’s working and what’s not working for them.  We would like input on the running of MCA and we want to get more people involved in MCA decision-making.  If you have questions that you would like to see asked in this questionnaire, email them to Kim Chanley.  Several topics that were brought up to go into this questionnaire were:  incentives for participation (supplemental insurance, paying people to come to training, for being on-call, etc.) and a monthly newsletter.

-         The board talked about having an additional informal meeting each month.  It would be open to whoever would like to attend.  Preliminary thoughts indicated we may try to have this extra meeting on a Sunday evening each month.

-         Kim will work on a new Mores Creek Ambulance brochure, highlighting the positive benefits for volunteers.

-         WE have anew jump kit for the ambulance.  Thank you to Karen Acker for putting it together and labeling it.  We would like to give the old jump kit away to an EMT, through a drawing, at the July general meeting.  Jo will email the members to tell them about this.

-         Jo attended the commissioners meeting about the Atlanta goldmine.  This project is still 2 years off.  Due to the extensive safety precautions that are being put into place, it is unlikely to increase our call volume, etc.  But it will go through Edna Creek Road, and thus through our territory and we will need to be aware of it.

-         We all need to work on bettering our PCR documentation.  The majority of our PCR’s are being sent back for corrections on simple things.  Please doublecheck all of your PCR’s before you hand them in for missing information, correctly filled-out bubbles, etc.

 

Motion to approve reports:  Approved
