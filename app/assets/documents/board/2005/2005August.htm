Advisory Board Report:

-We have a new Garmin GPS unit, intended to replace the computer that was in the ambulance.  Terry will work on getting it ready for use.

-PCR’s:  Remember to fill in mileage even when only transferring to EMS rendezvous.  Also, if you cannot get a signature, sign the form yourself and explain why.

-Blaine will take the old Bendix-King radios up at the next meeting.

-We do not have a deed yet to the property that Brad Stone is donating.  He will have architectural control of the building that will become a meeting hall/ambulance shed for our area.

-Idaho City is asking for EMT’s to volunteer to come up on Friday nights and help cover the high school football games.  Also, there are some JV football games during the week that need coverage as well.  Idaho City Ambulance will get us a schedule, and you can call Randy Smith if you are interested.  You will be using the Idaho City ambulance and equipment.

-Randy Smith from Idaho City Ambulance has invited any interested EMT’s or driver’s to trainings up at the Idaho City High School that will be held once a month on the 3rd Friday of the month.

-Idaho City is trying to put together an EMT class.  There will also be a class down at CPR Connections in September.  Please let a board member know if you are interested or know anyone who is.

-It is essential that you get a full receipt for any purchases made after a run.  The receipts must have a full accounting of what was purchased and the portable numbers written somewhere on it.  Please do not just get a stub with a total price.

-The EBCA meeting that was going to be held in Lowman in September will now be in October.

-The Rescue Me Class will be in November.  See Dawn Best for more information.

-We are still trying to get LifeBlankets for our unit.  Peggy will get the information from Blaine and submit what we want and the prices.

Special Board Meeting: August 15th, 2005

     -This meeting was informally called to discuss and try to solve the board issues.  *Peggy Blodgett is willing to step in as acting president for the present.  The membership will be asked to see if anyone else would like to fill the position of President. 

*Kim Chanley has agreed to stay on as Vice President. 

*We have decided to move our board meetings back to a different night then our general meetings.  They will be held prior to the EBCA meeting each month.  The September board meeting will be held on August 30th at 7:00pm at Peggy blodgett’s house.  All members are invited to attend. 

*We have made the September 13th general meeting a mandatory meeting.  It will be held on Tuesday, September 13th at 7:00pm at the ambulance shed, followed by training at 8:00pm.  Dinner will be provided for all members…we will be picking up a party sub, chips and soda.  Our unit is undergoing some significant changes and we would like the input and ideas and wishes of our members to guide us to the right decisions about the running and future of MCA.  Please make every effort to attend this meeting.
