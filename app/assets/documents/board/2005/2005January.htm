Mores Creek Ambulance Board Meeting

January 5th, 2005

 

Call to Order  7:06 pm

 

Board Members Present

     Joan McCarthy:  President

     Blaine Whitney:  Advisory Board Representative

     Kim Chanley:  Vice President

     Deb Arnswald:  Treasurer

     Molly Kaczmarek:  Secretary

 

Deb Arnswald is looking into donating our 2 pc’s to Computers for Kids.  We need to know such an action requires obtaining a release from EBCA.

 

The Advisory board meeting this month has been changed to January 13th.

 

We are trying to figure out how we wish to purchase storage units for the shed.  We would like to see if we can use money from the Homeland Security grant first.  If that is not available, we will go to EBCA if necessary.

 

We need an equipment officer and will ask for volunteers at one of our next meetings.  That person will then hold onto the numbered stickers that must be placed on any asset valued at over $100  (i.e. Stokes stretcher).

 

Dirk Myers and Dawn Best have volunteered for and have been appointed as our new Training Officers.

 

We would like to alter our training schedule to put more emphasis on training for our unit.  We are looking at continuing to hold our MCA board meeting on the first Wednesday of every month at the shed at 7:00pm.  Following this meeting, we will have our monthly general meeting at 8:00pm.  Training will be on the second Tuesdays of every month, and additional training will be on the following Saturday every other month.  These changes will tentatively go into effect in March, in order to give us ample time to properly prepare for such a schedule change.

 

 

Executive Session was called at 8:36 pm.

Executive Session ended at 8:46 pm.

 

We are going to be making a checklist for chores to be done around the shed, and we will put a rotating cleaning/maintenance schedule into effect.

 

The next board meeting will be held at Blaine Whitney’s house on Wednesday, February 2nd.  Any agenda items should be sent to Molly at least a week before the meeting.

 

 

Meeting adjourned at 9:12 pm.
