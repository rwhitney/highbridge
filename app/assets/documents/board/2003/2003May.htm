MORES CREEK AMBULANCE BOARD MEETING

Minutes for May 6, 2003

 

Terry Acker called the Regular Board Meeting of Mores Creek Ambulance Executive Board to order at 6:45 pm.  Board Members present: Terry Acker, John McCarthy, Peggy Blodgett and Cindy Drury.  Additional members present: Maureen.

 

Treasurer’s report- the balance in the checking account was $4172.64 on April 30, 2003.

 

New Vice President- everyone was emailed recently that Peggy Blodgett is the new VP for MCA.

 

Advisory Board Meeting- last week.   Provided a second KDE for the ambulance.  Anyone with patient contact must be inoculated and CPR trained.  County has no money to pay for the inoculations.  Meeting mostly spent in executive session.  Probably months before we have the laptop software.

 

Life Flight Memberships- Terry motioned that the unit should pay for Life Flight memberships, Maureen seconded and all approved.

 

Rummage Sale- Maureen raised the question of “What do we hope to buy with the proceeds from the rummage sale”?  We need to let the public know what we hope to buy.  Jennifer suggested: community education, CPR class, radios, and jumpsuits.

 

MCA SOP Update- Article 8- states that if MCA dissolved, all material possessions must go to another 501(c)(3).  Terry proposed that we submit the SOP Update to the membership next meeting, John seconded, and all members approved.

 

VP’s and Presidents Duties- Terry summarized the duties of VP and President for Peggy.  One duty of the VP is to handle new member applications- need driver record approval, references, interview information, e-mail if accepted into MCA, schedule driver training, schedule orientation.  Inform new applicants of the low call volume and a 12 hour shift requirement. Existing Applications in Progress- Paul Drury, Shena Labrum, Jim Armstrong.  Drivers have a $100 allotment for training and EMTs have a $150 allotment for training per year.

 

Peggy will cover the May General Meeting as Terry will be on vacation.

Vacations- John will be gone on vacation for a couple of weeks in June (June 6- ??).  John will also be very busy in June and may not be able to pull many shifts.  June 21st- Ride Tickets.

Terry made a motion to adjourn at 7:40 pm, Maureen seconded, and all members approved.
