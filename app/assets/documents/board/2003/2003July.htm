MORES CREEK AMBULANCE BOARD MEETING

Minutes for July 14, 2003

 

Terry Acker called the Regular Board Meeting of Mores Creek Ambulance Executive Board to order at 6:45 pm. 

 

New business

 

Radios- we have 3 types and most are narrow band compatible and will probably last for years.  It is difficult to gather for reprogramming.  Manufacturer charges at least $35 to reprogram each radio.  Terry is looking at purchasing equipment to reprogram them ourselves.  Would require a cable and software.

 

Fax and 2nd phone line- should we buy a fax machine for the shed so we can send and receive faxes from the hospital and others.  Terry proposed that MCA buy the fax machine and ask the county to pay for a second phone line.  Terry made a motion to buy a fax machine for approximately $200.00 and a lock for the laptop.  All approved.

 

Laptop update- Vickie had a death in the family so activity has been delayed.

 

New sheriff for Boise County- Drew Bodie.    Drew is a driver with MCA and is tuned in with EMS.

 

Member appreciation picnic August 9, 16, or 23.  We will put the date of the picnic to a vote at the member meeting.  Probably at about 2pm.  Bring toys for the water and perhaps a game of softball.

 

Terry made a motion to adjourn at 8:15 pm, John seconded, and all members approved.
