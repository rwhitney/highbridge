MORES CREEK AMBULANCE BOARD MEETING       

Minutes for January 8, 2003

 

Terry Acker called the Regular Board Meeting of Mores Creek Ambulance Executive Board to order at 6:40.  Board Members present: Terry Acker, Jennifer Haney, Nancy Cooper, John McCarthy, Cindy Drury, Maureen Ericksen.  Members present: Beth Chandler and Karen Acker.

 

Summary of last Board Meeting – Terry reviewed the outcome of the last board meeting.  The Board approved to buy a full size file cabinet for the Ambulance shed and to purchase Quicken 2003 for the unit.

 

Treasury Report- Jennifer is revising the report and adding some details and will have it completed tomorrow.

 

Advisory Board Meeting- summary by John.   County approved MCA to buy a gurney if  it costs less than $4,000.  A grant has been approved for MCA to purchase portable computers with GPS uplink that will automatically plot the location of the scene on computerized maps of Boise County.  They will be installed in the ambulance once Boise County enters the data (roughly 3 months).  A new medical director, Dr. Koenig of Garden Valley, has been hired and is very excited about the position.   Individuals taking the EMT class should be signed up with one of the Boise County ambulance units.

 

New Business presented by our new president, Terry Akers.

 

Schedule Master- Terry proposed two options: 1) We rotate the position between all members of MCA or 2) We find one individual that is willing to perform the role full-time.

 

Supply Manager- Maureen has volunteered to be the new supply manager.  Doug Trosky the former Supply manager has moved out of the immediate area.  Doug is still going to take care of Oxygen supplies.

 

New equipment- The County has approved the purchase of a new gurney (see notes above).  There is a new electronic thermometer in the ambulance for patient care.  Review how to use it if you are not sure.

 

Serviced Equipment- The automatic blood pressure cuff has been taken in for service.

 

New Members- There are three new members in MCA- Joanne McCarthy, Harry Blodget and Peggy Blodget.  All three are currently taking the EMT class in Idaho City!! We are currently interviewing other new members.

 

Teams and Team Leaders- We need to discuss the usefulness of teams at the general meeting.  Terry will summarize team leader responsibilities.

 

ID Cards- EMTs can get ID cards at Boise County Sheriffs Department for $5.00.  Jennifer volunteered to make IDs if the county approves. Karen is going to check into it.

 

The Shed- The hide-a-key is now located under the pot next to the front door.  We will be getting a new telephone in the ambulance shed since the only one currently in there in a cordless phone that does not work when the power goes out.  A motion was proposed and approved to purchase a pull down ladder.  The ladder will provide access to the attic area above the kitchen so it can be used for storage.  The Plexiglas light will be moved to make room.

 

Miscellaneous:  A motion was proposed and approved to renew the MCA internet domain name.  A motion was proposed and approved to present  Beth Chandler with a certificate for her contributions to the unit and for a gift certificate for dinner at Angles restaurant. 

 

Terry will give Jan Peterson, instructor of the EMT class, a list of applicants to MCA and applications for them to fill out.  They should turn the applications in to MCA, Jan Peterson (the instructor) or Cindy Drury.  Terry will also talk to Jan about what kind of help she needs with the EMT class from current EMTs.

 

Went into executive session at 7:25 until 7:40.

 

Last issue- Terry would like to strengthen the relationships between the ambulance units of East Boise County.  The current EMT training class in Idaho City is a good start. 

 

Meeting adjourned at 7:50.
