MORES CREEK AMBULANCE BOARD MEETING

Minutes for August 6, 2003

 

Terry Acker called the Regular Board Meeting of Mores Creek Ambulance Executive Board to order at 6:35 pm.  Maureen, Dave, Jennifer, Peggy, Cindy, John and Terry attending.

 

Treasurer’s report- the balance in the checking account was $3244.58 on July 30, 2003.  Plus we have a $250 check from Idaho Power and a $3000 check from the Robie Creek Race.

 

Old Business

  • Radio Programming- Terry purchased a cable.  There are issues with the software and Terry is still working on reprogramming the radios.  The problem with channels F1 & F2 on the ambulance radio has been fixed.

 

  • Picnic- Saturday at 2 pm.  Number of people: Placerville 8-12, RC Fire 12-15, WR Fire 8-10.  Will also invite Roger Jackson.  Dave, Terry and Peg will bring supplies.

 

  • Fax machine- a new Fax machine is in the ambulance shed and it is all hooked up.

 

  • Shirts- Jennifer will purchase T-shirts for new members plus a few extras.

 

  • Laptop- the laptop is in the ambulance. Most people have been trained.  The software is not very intuitive.  Try and use it so we can make suggestions of what we would like to see in the software.

 

New Business

 

  • Change of Date of Board Meeting-  Firefighter 1 training is going to be Wednesdays and every other Saturday.  The second Tuesday of the month is free for board meetings.

 

  • MCA service- any dependents in a MCA members household is covered for assessment or transportation in the ambulance/air at no charge.  Make sure you write “MCA family member- NO charge” on the PCR.

 

  • Rope training- Peggy & others will go and report back to MCA things that are relevant to us, such as steep slope rescue.  Terry will see if it is covered by our training budget.

 

  • SOP- for deceased patient.  Once deceased, Law enforcement or the coroner is responsible.  EBCA was on a call lately where the ambulance had to wait several hours for the coroner.  Peggy suggested that we need SOPs covering such.  John will bring up the issue at the advisory board meeting.

 

  • PCRs- make sure you write “Boise County resident” on the PCRs since many of us have Boise mailing addresses.  Patients from outside of Boise County get charged more.

 

Round table- nothing brought up.

 

Terry made a motion to adjourn at 7:40 pm, Jennifer seconded, and all members approved.
