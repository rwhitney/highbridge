MORES CREEK AMBULANCE BOARD MEETING

Minutes for March 10, 2003

 

Terry Acker called the Regular Board Meeting of Mores Creek Ambulance Executive Board to order at 6:45 pm.  Board Members present: Terry Acker, Karen Acker , John McCarthy, and Cindy Drury. 

 

Treasurer’s report- On February 28, 2003 the balance in the checking account was $4703.04.

 

Advisory board Meeting Report- Lasted 4 hours with 3 hours spent in executive session.

 

Laptop for ambulance- there is a meeting this Friday evening to discuss the software.

Life Flight Memberships- will be available soon.

Lowman is putting in a grant package for a new ambulance.

The ambulance will be in Boise this Tuesday, Wednesday or Thursday to get the computer mounted.

 

Robie creek Race- The Acker’s pasture will serve as the landing zone.  Terry is still trying to obtain clarification from the race officials regarding EMT activities.  Robie Creek Fire will also be there.  MCA gets approximately $1200.00 for being on standby during the race.

 

Fund raising activities- Should we have the rummage sale in June?  Terry will ask the membership when we should have it.

 

Equipment- Terry asked board members for any suggestions on what equipment is needed and could be included in a grant.  The following suggestions were made: radios, extraction equipment, specialized training (i.e. rescue), ropes, rack to lower people down obstacles, rescue harness, emergency red lights for vehicles, stair chair, lockers, aluminum cylinder for oxygen, and regulators.  Jumpsuits, shirts, sweatshirts, and MCA patches were suggested, but don’t qualify for grant items and need to be paid for with MCA funds.

 

MCA Bylaws – the following two changes need to be made: 1)change MCA to MCA Inc., and   2) clarification of what the board can do without a general membership vote. Terry and Jennifer will review for next meeting.

 

Leave of Absence- the following LOA were approved: Renee Johansen, Beth Chandler, Nancy Cooper, and Virginia Ware.

 

Training this month- Doug will cover the AED and  new gurney.  The board approved $20.00 to be spent on prizes for the AED training session.  The blue book was updated with Infection Control sheets.   Wilderness Ranch Extrication training on May 10th- MCA members can observe the training session.  Scavenger hunt- Karen will get started on planning it.

 

Terry made a motion to adjourn at 8:00 pm, John seconded.
