MORES CREEK AMBULANCE BOARD MEETING

Minutes for April 2, 2003

 

Terry Acker called the Regular Board Meeting of Mores Creek Ambulance Executive Board to order at 7:05 pm.  Board Members present: Terry Acker, Karen Acker , John McCarthy, and Cindy Drury.  Additional members present: Dave H. and Maureen.

 

Last months board minutes were circulated.  Terry made a motion to approve the minutes, and John seconded.

 

Treasurer’s report- the balance in the checking account is approximately $4800.  We spent approximately $500 for the CPA to review MCA Incorporation requirements and paperwork.

 

Advisory Board- no meeting last month to report on.

 

Schedule Master- Beth volunteered as Schedule Master for April.  We still need a Schedule Master for May.

 

Life Flight Membership will be approximately $7.54 per individual and $34.00 per family (it is normally $55.00 for an individual).  John will tell the county we will buy at least16, and an additional 16 if available.  We will vote on via e-mail whether the Unit will pay for the memberships as a gift for members, or if the members will pay (we will average the cost for all memberships). 

 

SOPs- Terry and Jennifer will modify and present the SOPs at the next meeting.  The SOPs will be emailed out and voted on.

 

Vice Present- Nancy Cooper stepped down from the VP position for the remainder of her term.  VP duties- responsible for the paperwork for new members, checking references and getting information to the county for driver checks, disciplinary actions, etcetera.

 

Jan Peterson- volunteered for the interim if we need her.  Other potential candidates: Beth, Kim, Maureen, Carolyn Bohn, Harry B., Peggy B., and Joan M.

 

Sponsorships- Maureen proposed that MCA sponsor Isaiah’s Ranch Water Safety Course for kids in the community.  The cost is $30 per child.  Jennifer motioned to approve a $300 donation, several members seconded.

 

Training- Doug Trosky willing to do a CPR class for the community and Jennifer indicated that she could get the Church to hold the class in.

 

Proposed Purchases

 

Maureen proposed the following:

  1. Buy jumpsuits for new members if they are on call more than 4 times per month.
  2. Purchase regulators for those who go out on a lot of calls.
  3. Buy a flashing red light to leave in the ambulance shed for the person on call.
  4. Rummage sale- Maureen will resend the email.

 

Red Lights- issue was brought up last month and board determined this is a gray area if the unit buys the lights.  Best if MCA does not buy the lights.

 

Regulators- we should looks for used regulators from hospitals, NW Paramedics, etcetera.

 

Jumpsuits- we can obtain jumpsuits from the army-navy surplus store and sew the appropriate patches on for a few dollars.   The jumpsuits would cost approximately $20 each and the patches/tape would cost about $15-$20.  Motion to buy jumpsuits was made and Maureen seconded.  The Board approved.

 

Round Table-

 

WR Extrication Training- changing date from May 10, due to a conflict with the EMT test.  The training will include multiple agencies.

 

T-shirts- We have a few available if anyone needs one.  Karen will e-mail everyone to see if they need one.

 

Training- Gus will conduct training on Infection Control at the next meeting.

 

Gurney- the new gurney is working well.  We will hold onto the old gurney until it is needed elsewhere.

 

Shed- John and Blaine will install stairs in the ambulance shed to get access to the attic for storage. 

 

Fuel for Ambulance- MCA can use the Wilderness Ranch Fire Department fuel tank to fuel the ambulance once in a while (i.e. the ambulance needs fuel, but is not in town).  John will see if the county will accept his bill for fuel.

 

Terry made a motion to adjourn at 8:05 pm, Jennifer seconded and all approved.
