MORES CREEK AMBULANCE BOARD MEETING NOTES

FEBRUARY 6, 2002

 

Start:  6:30 p.m.

 

Board members present:  Nancy Bishop, Beth Chandler, Rhonda Grindstaff, Dirk Myers

Officers present:  Karen Acker, Terry Acker

 

  • Reviewed minutes from January Board meeting.  Motion to approve by Dirk.  Second by Nancy.  Consensus to approve.
  • Teresa Snyder requested a Leave of Absence.  Motion to grant the LOA by Nancy, Second by Terry.  Majority approved.
  • Harry Chandler offered to help make updates to the bylaws and SOP’s.  Nancy will review and help draft final wording.  Karen will collect comments and put them together.
  • Dirk gave a fundraiser report.  There is some concern about the lack of enthusiasm for the car show.  There is a committee meeting February 15.  Dirk will send out an e-mail message to let the membership know and to solicit help.
  • John ordered the extrication air bags.  Boise County has agreed to pay the matching funds.  The air bags should arrive at Beth’s any day.  SOP’s regarding safe operation and use have to be in place shortly after the unit takes possession of the bags.
  • We had a brief discussion regarding the ambulance fuel level being between ½ and ¾ full.  Remind the membership to fuel the ambulance when going to Idaho City and Boise.  Let John or Terry know if you need fuel.
  • Terry volunteered to find out if we need to keep a mileage book.
  • Board members signed the signature card for the checking account to allow Beth and Nancy to use it.
  • Nancy made a motion to adjourn into an Executive Session and Terry seconded it.
  • The following topics were discussed for the Good of the Order/Miscellaneous:
    • Setting up a reward system for members or others who go above and beyond the call of duty
    • Dirk will ask Team Leaders to contact members again for missing certification information
    • Some members on Leave of Absence are approaching 6 months.  Terry will handle this as Personnel Officer.

 

 

The meeting adjourned after 9:00 p.m.
