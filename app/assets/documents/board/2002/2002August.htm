MORES CREEK AMBULANCE BOARD MEETING

AUGUST 13, 2002

 

Meeting called to order at 6:35 by Terry Acker

 

BOARD MEMBERS PRESENT: Karen Acker (proxy for Beth Chandler), Terry Acker, Nancy Bishop

 

OTHER MEMBERS PRESENT: Margie Jones

 

MINUTES: from July’s meeting approved.

 

TREASURER’S REPORT: approved, also on website

 

STATE EMS GRANT:  Margie reported that we have gotten tentative approval for the laptop and software, also possible approval for EMT classes.  Lowman ambulance was not approved.

 

Doug Trosky requested LOA was approved.

 

RECOGNITION BANQUET: Margie Jones and Maureen Ericksen will be handing out the invitations for the banquet at the general meeting, mailing the rest to the members.  The banquet will be held at Louie’s restaurant. RSVP’s must respond by Sept. 7th.  The cost will be $12.90 per person, paid in advance.  Margie requested an additional $100 to be spent on misc. items for the party, Terry so motioned, Nancy seconded, approved.

 

TRAINING EVENTS:  This months general meeting will be O2 administration by Doug Trosky.  We will also be having a scavenger hunt on September 7th with a blood drive following the scavenger hunt.  Jan Peterson will be teaching splinting for the September general meeting.

 

BOARD MEETING TIMES/DATE:  It was suggested to change the board meeting day to the 2nd Tuesday of the month.  It was tabled until more input has been received.

 

GENERAL NEWS:

 

*Life Flight needs two months of notice on their renewal.  Nancy Bishop will look into this.

*Firefighters injured on the job need to contact billing/Treasea Long to find out if any consideration will       be given for their bill.

*The O2 bag zipper was non-functional and a new bag was considered.  Margie Jones will look into             having a new zipper installed in the bag.

*Communication after a call is transferred to ALS was discussed.  There appears to be no standard of             communication between ALS and MCA.  It was discussed that the lead EMT should be the one             to call for information so there isn’t a multitude of calls made to the agencies.  Also discussed             was to call the agencies and ask them for their input on this matter.

*We will need an interim medical supply officer while Doug Trosky is on LOA.

*Ropes Training is tentatively cancelled for this fall.

 

NEXT MEETING: General meeting August 15th at the MCA shed at 6:45. Topic will be O2 administration.

 

ADJOURN: With no further business Terry moved, Nancy seconded, motion carried and the meeting was adjourned at 8:15pm.
