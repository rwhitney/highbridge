Call to Order  6:45 pm

 

Board Members Present

This meeting was largely held to discuss issues about the changing of the board, and to pass on old information that is pertinent for the new board members.

 

·        Terry will print out relevant e-mails that he has received for the new president.

·        We will be looking at finding a training officer and supply officer.

·        Our Mores Creek checking account statements get sent to the Mores Creek mailbox.  We will have the new Treasurer and new President get authorized to sign on the checking account.

·        Treasurer needs to look into the reimbursements for people who have completed their first year with Mores Creek Ambulance.

·        We would like to get more EMS license plates and/or EMS stickers...’Signs’ in Garden City is one place that does them.

·        There is not much warning when the Annual Budget meeting for EBCA happens, so we need to start thinking about budget issues and have a list ready by March so that we are prepared.

·        There is a Rim View Annual Meeting that is good to go to for good-neighbor relations.  It is held sometime in May.

 

Executive Session was called at 7:05 pm.

Executive Session ended at 7:25 pm.

 

·        Terry Acker has offered to continue doing website support for MCA.  He is also willing to continue doing ambulance maintenance if no one else is available.

·        John McCarthy has been doing the shed maintenance.  Some of the current issues are; a pipe to protect the new furnace, better illumination in garage, propane cover, grading driveway.

·        There are 2 PC’s, one donated by Beth Chandler and one donated by Virginia Ware.  We need to find out if our ‘townies’ use the computer to any extent and if it is worth keeping up there, or do we need to find a new one.

·        Fund-raising efforts have flattened out.  We need more community visibility.

·        The status of Epipen’s is still uncertain.

·        We are still having problems getting PCR’s turned in.

·        Peggy has the new supplies at her house...we need to find an appropriate spot in the shed to store them where they won’t freeze.

·        The MCA laptop computer needs to get updated.

·        There is no space on the ambulance for the Stokes litter, and we need to consider the possibility of putting it on the WRVFD crash truck.

·        We still have 4 available new radios.  Terry is willing to continue programming the radios.

·        Joan will write up a letter to the advisory board with a list of the newly-appointed board members.

 

Meeting adjourned at 8:34 pm.
