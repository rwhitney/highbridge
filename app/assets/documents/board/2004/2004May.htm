Those in attendance were Kim Chanley, Jennifer Haney, Jan Peterson and Peggy Blodgett.

Treasurer’s report not available, will be brought to the General Meeting for approval. Terry will email a copy of it to the members prior to the meeting to give everyone time to review it.

 

Advisory Board Meeting Report

Nothing significant to report

 

Old Business/New Business

§         Jan Jackson, the wife of Roger Jackson (County Commissioner) has passed away. A sympathy card was signed by the board and will be delivered to Roy at the memorial service in Idaho City by Jan Peterson.  Additionally, a donation in the amount of $50 was made on behalf of Mores Creek Ambulance to the Idaho City Community Club as requested in lieu of flowers.

§         The board also signed a card to Mike at St. Luke’s thanking him for his assistance in fixing our BP machine

§         Terry attended the meeting of the Rim View Estates homeowners association to learn about the gate to be installed (no progress on that) and to respond to two complaints of MCA members driving too fast on the roads.  Terry asked for specific information for follow up which has not been provided to date.  Additionally, the homeowners association is asking for each road-front property owner to pay $160 up front for dust abatement to be reimbursed later by Boise County.  The board voted in favor of paying the fee.

§         Ambulance/Shed maintenance: Terry has removed the chains and will send Phyllis a note to ask if new summer tires are in the budget. Terry has provided a list of things that need to be maintained or fixed at the shed and the list will be presented to the general meeting.

§         Jennifer has mailed the design for the patches to the company making them; they will give us a quote for 100 patches before they make them.

§         Peg will contact Dr. Sturky about his interest in being MCA’s medical director and Jan will contact the VA.

§         Training officer: Joan McCarthy is our new training officer and will begin attending our board meetings.

§         Board meeting days have been changed from the first Tuesday of the month to the first Wednesday of the month starting with the June meeting.

 

Adjourned.
