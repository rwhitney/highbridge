MCA BOARD MEETING MINUTES FEBRUARY 19, 2006

 

Carolyn Bohn called the board meeting of MCA to order at 7:00 pm.  Meeting was held at MCA building, officers present: Carolyn Bohn, Dirk Myers, Sheena Labrum, Karen Acker and Peg Blodgett.  Other members present were Dawn Best, Jennifer Haney, David Haney, and Jason Christiansen.

 

John McCarthy and Jeff Arnswald from WRFPD were present to give presentation on First Response District.  They will be creating a first response area in their fire district.  They will be speaking to EMSAC on March 16th at 4:30 about the creation of this district’s First Response area.

Not all members of WRFPD will be qualified as first responders, but to be in the WRFPD you must be a fire fighter to be a first responder.
